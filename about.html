ABOUT

Anarres is a creative residency established in Skardu, Pakistan. The goal of the residency is to provide a provide a sanctuary for artists, musicians, writers, and remote workers seeking inspiration in the breathtaking landscape of Baltistan. Through a holistic living experience, we foster creative freedom, promote sustainability, and facilitate cultural exchange. Our mission extends to uplifting the local community by preserving indigenous heritage and enhancing the skills of local artisans.

CORE VALUES

Creativity & Expression

Nurturing artistic freedom and providing the space and resources for all creative disciplines.

Sustainability

Embracing organic farming, eco-conscious living, and minimal environmental impact.

Community & Collaboration

Fostering a supportive network for artists, artisans, and adventurers to connect and create.

Serenity & Focus

Offering a peaceful retreat to rejuvenate and find clarity away from the distractions of modern life.

Cultural Preservation

Actively engaging in the documentation and celebration of local music, culture, food, and artisanal crafts.
