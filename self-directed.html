SELF-DIRECTED RESIDENCIES

Anarres offers self-directed residencies to artists, musicians, writers, painters, filmmakers, scientists, humanists, and all creatives alike. The residence houses two individual bedrooms with attached bathroom. Each room can house 1-2 individuals. This is a paid residency program. We do not provide any stipend or scholarship to individuals looking to apply for the self-directed residency.
