offers a creative, contemplative, and safe space to pursue artistic or scholarly projects in the breathtaking landscape of Skardu, Pakistan. We offer a holistic living experience that combines creative freedom with organic, self-sustaining practices, fostering an environment where artistic endeavors thrive.

We offer self-directed residencies along with artist-led residencies and retreats throughout the year.

We welcome applications from artists, musicians, filmmakers, painters, writers, scientists, humanists, and remote workers in all fields who are looking to reconnect with nature and rejuvenate.

Residencies

We offer self-directed and artist-led residencies throughout the year. Applications for self-directed residencies are accepted on a rolling basis. Artist-led residencies are offered at set dates and applications are accepted during a limited time frame.

Self-Directed

Self-directed residencies are for individuals planning on staying for at least one week or more. The residence offers private accommodation with an attached bathroom and shared studio spaces amidst a beautiful orchard comprising numerous fruit trees and organic vegetation. The residence is equipped with grid and backup electricity, stable optic fibre internet, running hot water, and all necessary amenities. Each bedroom can house 1-2 individuals.

Artist-Led

We offer numerous artist-led residencies ranging from arts to adventure. They include (but are not limited to) music, film, organic farming, and climbing. Each of these residencies are led by seasoned professionals and are aligned with the goals of knowledge transfer and skill acquisition.
